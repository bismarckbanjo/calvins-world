Calvin Hop

A tiny Calvin platformer focused on jump feel.

Tap / press jump for a short hop, hold for a full jump, and jump again in the air for a double-jump.

Blue platforms move. Purple cracked platforms crumble after you touch them. Calvin now uses separate standing/running legs plus a stretched airborne leg sprite.

Press jump, tap the screen, or hit Space to start.
